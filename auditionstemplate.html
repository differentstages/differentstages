4000 Miles
by Amy Herzog


WHEN and WHERE: Monday, October 12 and Monday, October 19 from 7pm – 9pm
Dougherty Arts Center, 1110 Barton Springs Rd

PLOT: After suffering a major loss while he was on a cross-country bike trip, 21 year-old Leo seeks solace from his feisty 91 year-old grandmother, Vera, in her West Village apartment. Over the course of a single month, these unlikely roommates infuriate, bewilder, and ultimately reach each other.

4000 MILES looks at how two outsiders find their way in today's world.
ROLES: 1 MAN AGE 18-26
2 WOMEN 18-26 (one role is ASIAN-AMERICAN)
The role of Vera will be played by Jennifer Underwood.
PRODUCTION DETAILS: 
March 11 – April 2, 2016 at Ground Floor Theatre
